## Content Strategy

### Money Pages (Transactional)
- Directory, Join, List Practice
- Optimize for conversion, clear CTAs
- Target high-intent keywords

### SEO Magnets (Informational)  
- Articles, Resources, Events
- Build topical authority
- Capture long-tail traffic → funnel to money pages

### Internal Linking
- Every article should link to Join or List Practice
- Resources page links to all articles
- Homepage features top articles
